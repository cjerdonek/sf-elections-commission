The San Francisco Elections Commission oversees all public elections in San Francisco. It sets general policies for the Department of Elections and is responsible for the proper administration of its general practices. See About Us for more information.

Upcoming Meetings

The full Commission meets on the 3rd Wednesday of each month. Budget and Oversight of Public Elections Committee (BOPEC) meetings are the 1st Wednesday of each month but does not necessarily meet each time.

The latest information on upcoming meetings appears below. Cancellation notices for regularly scheduled meetings also appear below. The list below is subject to change, for example if a special meeting is added or a regular meeting is canceled.
