The City Charter of San Francisco authorizes the Elections Commission to supervise the City's Department of Elections. The Commission sets general policies for the Department: it also selects and supervises the Director of the Department of Elections.


The Elections Commission has regularly scheduled meetings on the third Wednesdays of each month at 6:00 pm, City Hall, Room 408. Special Meetings may be called as needed. There are also committee meetings. Please check the Meetings page of this website for more information or to learn if a scheduled meeting is cancelled.
