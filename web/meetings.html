The Elections Commission usually meets the third Wednesday of each month in City Hall, Room 408 at 6:00 pm. The Commission's Budget and Oversight of Public Elections Committee (BOPEC) usually meets the first Wednesday of each month in City Hall, Room 421 at 6:00 pm.

Please check agendas for meeting times and locations.

For information on obtaining meeting materials, see Meeting Materials. For information about attending meetings, including how to speak at a meeting, see Attending Meetings. For information about disability access to meetings, see Disability Access.

For audio recordings of meetings, see Audio Recordings. For earlier meetings, see Earlier Meetings.
