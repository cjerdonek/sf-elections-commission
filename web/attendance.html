Seat Key

  • BE = Board of Education
  • BS = Board of Supervisors
  • CA = City Attorney
  • DA = District Attorney
  • Ma = Mayor
  • PD = Public Defender
  • Tr = Treasurer

Attendance Key

  • P = Present
  • EA = Excused Absence
  • UA = Unexcused Absence
  • L = Late
  • EL = Excused Late
  • UL = Unexcused Late
  • EE = Early Exit
  • V = Vacant seat
  • – = Attendance not required (e.g. meeting canceled or not a committee member)
